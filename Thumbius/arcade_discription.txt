This is a remake of a spooky urban legend of the killer arcade game POLYBIUS.
It keeps most of the notable features including the ship, the enemies, and the strange rotating map.

INSTRUCTIONS

Controls:

- Use WASD or D-Pad to move your space ship (the triangle) across the screen

- Press A to shoot

Gameplay:

- Dodge the solid squares that move around the center hexagon
- Shoot the sonic squares to move on to the next phase

- Every phase gets faster and with more enemies

- The amount of enemies to defeat increases every phase

Have fun playing!
