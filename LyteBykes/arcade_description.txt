LyteBykes

Inspired by an arcade classic, in LyteBykes
the object is to cut off the gray AI bike and make it
crash into one of the light trails or walls.

The first bike to score 5 wins is the overall winner!

# of Players: 1 (multiplayer version is in progress)

Controls: Use Up, Left, Down, and Right buttons to 
control your bike.

Author: PanduhBeer
Version: 1.0
