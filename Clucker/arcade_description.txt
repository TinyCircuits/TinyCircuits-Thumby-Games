Clucker

Why did the chicken cross the road?
Who Knows?
Anyway, enjoy this interactive dramatization of the act.
See if you can get Chicken across the road ten times in a row
and celebrate the achievement for yourself!

# of Players: 1

Controls: Simple as can be...
Use Up, Left, Down, and Right buttons to 
control Chicken.
